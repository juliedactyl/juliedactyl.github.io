About Julie

I am originally from Norway, but am currently based in Dublin, Ireland. In 2022 I completed my Master's in Nanotechnology at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), and if you're interested you can find my thesis here.

After many wonderful years in Trondheim I embarked on the PhD journey of a lifetime, and I am now happily working in the Ultramicroscopy group at Trinity College Dublin. Supervised by the brilliant Prof. Lewys Jones, I'm working with detector signal digitisation for ultra low-dose phase contrast imaging in scanning transmission electron microscopy.

When I'm not busy thinking about my work, I enjoy various kinds of sports and outdoor activities including trampolining, running and hiking. Still, my number 1 hobby for years is photography.
